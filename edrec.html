Ed Banger Records

Ed Banger Records was founded in 2003 by Pedro Winter as a division of Headbanger Entertainment.

The label mainly focuses on the French House genre of music. Other noteable artists signed to the label include: Justice, Sebastian, Mr. Flash, Mr. Oizo, and even Pedro Winter himself under the alias Busy P.
